title: "ZN"
permalink: "/z/zn/"
definitions:
  - definition: |
      Abbreviation of “Zona Norte”, the northern part of a city, state, country. In São Paulo it's
      quite common to use it as the city is divided into four zones (northern, southern, eastern, western)
      and the city centre.
    example: |
      - Aonde fica Santana?
      - Fica na **ZN**.
