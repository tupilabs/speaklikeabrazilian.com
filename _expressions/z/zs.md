title: "ZS"
permalink: "/z/zs/"
definitions:
  - definition: |
      Abbreviation of “Zona Sul”, the southern part of a city, state, country. In São Paulo it's
      quite common to use it as the city is divided into four zones (northern, southern, eastern, western)
      and the city centre.
    example: |
      - Aonde fica o Morumbi?
      - Fica na **ZS**.
